Dr. Jordan B Peterson. Professor of psychology at university of Toronto

    Here's some information about Dr. Peterson

  • 1962 - Born in Fairview, Alberta
  • 1979 - Graduated from Fairview High School
  • 1984 - Received B.A. in psychology from University of Alberta
  • 1985 - Moved to Montreal to attend McGill University
  • 1991 - Earned his Ph.D in clinical psychology
  • 1993 - Started teaching at Harvard University as an associate professor in psychology department
  • 1998 - Returned to Canada and started working as a professor at University of Toronto

    Published Books

  • Maps of Meaning: The Architecture of Belief (1999)
  • 12 Rules for Life: An Antidote to Chaos (2018)
